PIP: A social token.

About PIP

PIP is a social token that I created on the Solana blockchain. Solana's fast and cost-effective blockchain technology ensures that transactions involving PIP tokens happen seamlessly. This means quick and affordable interactions within the PIP community. While other tokens on the Solana blockchain are created with real-world functionality in mind, PIP was created out of curiosity and a general desire to learn more about blockchain development. At the moment, PIP is exclusive to a short list of authorized holders.

See PIP on Solscan

PIP's design is inspired by Frutiger Aero, the dominating design language of the tech world between 2004 and 2013.
